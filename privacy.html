1. Who We Are

Healthy-E is an online wellness and organic products store operated by NC-Developer, LLC, a limited liability company organized and existing under the laws of the State of Maryland, owned and managed by Neeka Chisholm.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you visit Healthy-E Store (the "Site") or make a purchase from us. Please read this policy carefully. By using the Site, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

Governing Authority

NC-Developer, LLC is formed under the Maryland Limited Liability Company Act, Md. Code Ann., Corps. & Ass'ns §§ 4A-101 et seq. This policy is further governed by the Maryland Consumer Protection Act, Md. Code Ann., Com. Law §§ 13-101 to 13-501.

2. Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide Directly

  • Name, email address, phone number, and billing/shipping address
  • Payment information (processed securely; we do not store full card numbers)
  • Account credentials if you create an account
  • Communications you send us (support requests, reviews, feedback)

B. Information Collected Automatically

  • IP address, browser type, operating system, and referring URLs
  • Pages visited, time spent, click patterns, and cart activity
  • Cookie identifiers and similar tracking technologies (see our Cookie Policy)
Maryland Personal Information Protection Act (MPIPA)

Pursuant to Md. Code Ann., Com. Law §§ 14-3501 to 14-3508, "personal information" means an individual's first name or first initial and last name, combined with any one or more of the following: Social Security number, driver's license number, financial account number, or medical information. We collect and protect such data in full compliance with MPIPA.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process and fulfill your orders and send related communications
  • Manage your account and provide customer support
  • Send promotional emails and newsletters (with your consent; you may opt out at any time)
  • Analyze site usage to improve our products and services
  • Comply with applicable Maryland and federal laws and regulations
  • Detect and prevent fraud or unauthorized activity
Maryland Online Privacy Protection Act (MOPPA)

Under Maryland's Online Privacy Protection Act and the Maryland Consumer Protection Act (Md. Code Ann., Com. Law § 13-301), we do not engage in unfair, abusive, or deceptive data practices. We will never sell your personal information to third-party marketers without your explicit consent.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share your data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Payment processors, shipping carriers, email marketing platforms, and analytics tools engaged to support our operations — each bound by confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal Compliance: When required by Maryland or federal law, court order, subpoena, or to protect the rights and safety of NC-Developer, LLC or others.
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, subject to the same privacy protections.
Maryland Breach Notification Law — Md. Code Ann., Com. Law § 14-3504

In the event of a breach of the security of a system that contains personal information, NC-Developer, LLC will notify affected Maryland residents in the most expedient time possible and without unreasonable delay, in accordance with Maryland law.

5. Your Maryland Privacy Rights

As a Maryland resident, you have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to Know: You may request disclosure of the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you.
  • Right to Delete: You may request that we delete personal information we collected from you, subject to certain exceptions.
  • Right to Correct: You may request correction of inaccurate personal information.
  • Right to Opt-Out: You may opt out of receiving promotional communications at any time.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of these rights.
Maryland Consumer Protection Act — Md. Code Ann., Com. Law § 13-301

The Maryland Consumer Protection Act prohibits unfair or deceptive trade practices in the collection and use of consumer information. NC-Developer, LLC is fully compliant with these provisions and maintains transparent data practices.

To exercise any of the above rights, contact us at privacy@nc-developer.com or by mail at the address listed in Section 9. We will respond within 45 days as required by Maryland law.

6. Data Security

We implement commercially reasonable technical, administrative, and physical safeguards to protect your personal information, including SSL/TLS encryption for data in transit, access controls, and secure payment processing through PCI-DSS compliant providers.

Maryland Personal Information Protection Act — Security Requirements

Pursuant to Md. Code Ann., Com. Law § 14-3503, NC-Developer, LLC implements and maintains reasonable security procedures and practices appropriate to the nature of the personal information it handles, to protect that information from unauthorized access, destruction, use, modification, or disclosure.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, or as required by applicable Maryland or federal law. Order records are retained for a minimum of 7 years for tax and accounting purposes under Maryland law. You may request deletion of your data subject to these legal retention obligations.

8. Children's Privacy

The Healthy-E Store is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent, in compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), 15 U.S.C. §§ 6501–6506. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.
