We started Healthy-E because we believed premium nutrition shouldn't mean compromising on the planet or your values.

Where It All Began

In 2022, Healthy-E was born out of a simple frustration: finding truly clean, ethically sourced wellness products was harder than it should be. Labels were misleading, supply chains were opaque, and "organic" had become a marketing buzzword more than a promise.

We set out to change that. Starting with a small collection of ceremonial-grade matcha and cold-pressed superfoods, we built direct relationships with farmers in Japan, Peru, and Costa Rica — people who share our obsession with quality and our commitment to sustainable land stewardship.

What began as a passion project has grown into a full wellness ecosystem: yoga apparel made from recycled materials, premium nutrition staples, and fitness equipment designed to last — all united by the same uncompromising standard.

"We believe the best thing you can do for yourself
and the planet are the same thing."

Our Mission

Healthy-E exists to make premium, sustainable wellness accessible — without the greenwashing. Every product in our catalog is evaluated against three criteria: Is it the best quality available? Is it sourced responsibly? And does it genuinely support a healthier life?

We publish the sourcing story behind every product, so you always know exactly what you're putting in your body and who grew it. No proprietary blends, no hidden fillers, no vague "natural flavors."

Radical Transparency

Full sourcing stories, third-party lab results, and honest ingredient lists on every product page.

Living Wages First

We pay above fair-trade minimums and visit every farm partner annually to ensure standards are kept.

Planet Positive

Carbon-neutral shipping, compostable packaging, and 1% of every sale donated to reforestation.
